# PureShot Site Design Contract

- Keep the site website-only. Do not modify the iOS app or app screenshots from this repo.
- Use the current app icon for favicon, Apple touch icon, brand marks, and compact navigation.
- Visual direction: restrained camera-lab editorial, with muted material surfaces, sharp copy, and real app screenshots as proof.
- Navigation should expose App, Blog, Support, Privacy, and Terms where context allows; homepage may also include Capture and Screens anchor links.
- Blog surfaces should feel human and opinionated, but must stay grounded in implemented PureShot launch behavior.
- Screenshots must remain inspectable and unblurred. Do not crop them into generic decoration when they are product evidence.
- Keep cards purposeful: individual proof items, blog/support resources, and legal/support sections. Avoid nested cards and decorative clutter.
- Use `pureshot@mesikalabs.com` for app support, `privacy@mesikalabs.com` for privacy, `billing@mesikalabs.com` for billing, and `legal@mesikalabs.com` for legal.
