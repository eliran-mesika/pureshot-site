What If a No-Filter Camera App Just Took the Picture?

A weird idea, apparently: open the camera, point it at reality, and do not immediately smear the entire scene with algorithmic frosting. PureShot is for people who want a no-filter camera app because they already own a face and do not need software to rent them a new one.

Filters are not always features.

Sometimes they help. Often they make skin look like a wax museum got a firmware update. PureShot keeps the capture surface focused on controls, resolution, zoom, and local handling instead of pretending every photo needs a makeover montage.

Local first, because photos are personal.

The launch app keeps media local unless you export through iOS. No account maze. No tracking SDK. No cloud upload hiding behind a friendly gradient button.
