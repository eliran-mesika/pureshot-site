PureShot support

PureShot V1 is a fully free iPhone camera app for iPhone 15 and newer, focused on Process Zero capture, manual controls, and local-only media handling.

If a feature is unavailable on a specific device, PureShot shows an explicit diagnostic fallback instead of silently hiding the limitation.

For video, start with the 180-degree rule: use a shutter speed close to double the frame rate, then adjust intentionally for the scene.

Support contact: open a public support issue.
