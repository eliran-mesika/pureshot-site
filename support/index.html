Support Manual

Operate with intent.

PureShot V1 is a fully free iPhone camera for iPhone 15 and newer, focused on Process Zero capture, manual controls, and local-only media handling.

Device support

Baseline capture supports iPhone 15 and newer. Apple Log, ProRes-oriented workflows, and external SSD indicators are available only where supported hardware and OS capabilities allow them.

Unavailable features

If a feature is unavailable on a device, PureShot shows a diagnostic fallback instead of silently hiding the limitation.

Manual exposure

Use manual exposure controls to hold ISO and shutter behavior when the active camera exposes those controls.

Manual focus

Manual focus is available when the active iPhone camera supports locked custom lens positioning.

180-degree rule

For natural video motion, start with shutter speed near double the frame rate, then adjust intentionally for the scene.
