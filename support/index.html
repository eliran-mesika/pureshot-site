Support Manual

Capture support without clutter.

PureShot V1 is a fully free camera app for iPhone 15 and newer plus M-series iPad models, focused on Process Zero capture, direct shooting controls, advanced profiles, and local-only media handling.

Device support

Baseline capture supports iPhone 15 and newer plus M-series iPad models. Apple Log, ProRes-oriented workflows, and external SSD indicators are available only where supported hardware and OS capabilities allow them.

Resolution

Use the visible resolution control for common Photo and Video format choices. Auto remains the safest default when the active camera limits available formats.

Lens zoom

Pinch on the viewfinder or use the compact lens chips for common 0.5x, 1x, and 2x zoom behavior. Detailed Lens controls remain in Profiles.

Profiles

The Profiles panel exposes Photo, Video, Lens, and Capabilities details without crowding the main camera surface.

Unavailable features

If a feature is unavailable on a device, PureShot shows a clear fallback instead of silently hiding the limitation.
