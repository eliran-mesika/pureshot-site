Privacy Policy

Zero tracking. Local processing.

PureShot does not collect personal data, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, and does not upload captured media to a cloud service.

Photos, videos, and capture metadata stay on the device unless the user explicitly exports through iOS system flows.

Camera access is used to show the viewfinder and capture media. Microphone access is requested only for video capture flows that need audio.

For support, contact the developer through the support page.
